Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Comfy Portal. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Server Connection Data: ComfyUI server addresses that you input for connection purposes
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how you use the app to improve our service
  • Device Information: Basic device information required for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Facilitating connections to your ComfyUI servers
  • Improving app functionality and user experience
  • Providing technical support

Data Storage

All server connection information is stored locally on your device. We do not store any of your ComfyUI server data or generated images on our servers.

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for analytics and crash reporting. These services collect minimal data and comply with standard privacy practices.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Delete your data from the app
  • Opt-out of analytics collection

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
